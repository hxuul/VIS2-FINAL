The first visualization illustrates the Distribution of Payment Types Across Age Groups. It breaks down various social service payment types such as Age Pension, Commonwealth Rent Assistance, Pension Concession Card, and others, showing their distribution across different age groups. The graph highlights that the largest number of beneficiaries falls within the 65 and over category, with Age Pension dominating that segment.

description

The second visualization shows the Distribution of Payment Count Across Age Groups by State, with an additional Total Count by State. The upper area chart represents how different age groups are distributed across Australian states. It demonstrates a concentration of older age groups in the 70-74 and 75-79 brackets, with a sharp decline in the population count as the age increases to 90 and over. The Total Count by State bar chart below shows that New South Wales and Victoria have the highest overall counts of payment recipients, while smaller states and territories like Northern Territory and Tasmania show relatively lower total counts.
